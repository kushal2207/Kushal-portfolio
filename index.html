Sliding Bracket

A sliding bracket that taught me tolerancing the hard way.

Fusion 360 Tolerancing Functional fit

A compact bracket with a U-shaped sliding channel and two screw-mount holes on the top face. Designed with adjustable slot tolerances to explore mechanical fit, clearance, and assembly constraints. Stepped profile with chamfered edges built for real-world manufacturability.

Mobile Phone Stand

A phone stand that prints correctly on the first try.

Fusion 360 3D Print-ready Ergonomic angle

Pillar-style stand with a cylindrical column, pivoting cradle, and a chamfered hexagonal base. Filleted corner cutouts and a flared column-to-base transition for load distribution. Designed for desktop use and 3D printing—focused on ergonomic viewing angle and a clean revolve-and-fillet workflow.

Headphone & Cable Holder

A desk holder that finally made cables behave.

Fusion 360 Swept profiles Joint modeling

Dual-arm desk holder with two rounded vertical arms joined at a pivot-hinge joint, mounted on a triangular looped base. Explored hinge-and-joint modelling and swept profiles to achieve the organic rounded arm geometry.
